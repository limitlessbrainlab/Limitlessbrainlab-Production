It tells you which strategies will actually work for your brain.

How we determined your type

    Your qEEG showed signatures that map onto the Sentinel (Type 5) profile, with secondary Empath features:
    • Right-shifted frontal alpha asymmetry (-16.55) — a core Sentinel marker. Your brain is wired for threat-monitoring,
      anticipatory worry, and vigilance. The emotional and creative centers run in the background.
    • High arousal (1.58) with critically low relaxation (4.53) — the nervous system is locked in 'on' mode. This is the Sentinel
      signature: prepared, driven, but unable to rest.

    • Severe stress dysregulation (10%) despite a healthy cognitive engine — unlike most Sentinel-type brains, yours isn't
      collapsing under pressure, it's collapsing in recovery. You can perform, but not sustain.

      brain is running a double load: vigilance circuits monitoring threat, while emotional sensitivity amplifies reaction. Sleep is
