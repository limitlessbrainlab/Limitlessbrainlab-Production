SECTION 7 — YOUR PERSONALIZED PLAN


Your 30-day brain plan
Your 30-day plan targets your core problem: a nervous system locked in high alert that cannot access
recovery. Small daily inputs to lower arousal and build relaxation will compound fast — your healthy
alpha peak means you have the cognitive wiring to respond.

Week-by-week build
     WEEK 1 — CALM FIRST
     Lock in the daily anchors
     Just the anchors above. Prove to your nervous system that calm is safe and consistent.

     W E E K 2 — C O N TA I N
     Add structure & containment
     A short evening wind-down and a 3-item morning priority list. Contain the open loops.

     WEEK 3 — RECOVER
     Add one weekly true-rest session
     90 minutes of no productivity, no input, no goal. It will feel uncomfortable — that is the point.

     W E E K 4 — A C T I VAT E
     Layer in performance work
     Add a daily goal-activation routine — one intention plus one thing you are looking forward to.



    ◷ After 30 days
    Relaxation and regeneration scores typically shift first — expect easier wind-downs within 2-3 weeks and clearer mornings as
    recovery starts to catch up with output. Stress regulation and emotional bandwidth follow as your baseline arousal lowers.
