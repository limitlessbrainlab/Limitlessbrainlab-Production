Cognition & stress
These are the two engines of daily performance — how clearly you think and how well you handle pressure. Together
they determine whether your brain is helping you or working against you.



    Why your stress runs high
    Your high arousal (1.58) sits alongside weak stress regulation and severe burnout markers — the opposite of a "compensated" Sentinel. The
    driven, vigilant wiring is here, but the recovery that normally balances it has run out, so the same arousal that should fuel performance is instead
    feeding insomnia, reactivity and fatigue.

    The good news: this is the most reversible pattern of all. Your job isn't to lower your drive — it's to rebuild the recovery and add the regulation
    buffer your current system is missing. Stress and burnout scores tend to move first when recovery habits go in.
