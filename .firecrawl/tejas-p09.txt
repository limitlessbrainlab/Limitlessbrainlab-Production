    The hidden link between these three
    Emotional regulation, creative thinking and durable learning all depend on the same underlying state: low arousal + alert alpha. When the
    nervous system runs hot and depleted, all three drop together. When you give the brain real recovery, all three rise — usually together. That's why
    the plan focuses on calming and recovering, not on adding more.
