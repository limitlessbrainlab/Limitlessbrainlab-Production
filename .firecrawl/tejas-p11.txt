Week-by-week build

    WEEK 1 — RECOVER FIRST
    Lock in the four daily anchors + one daily reset
    Just the four habits above, plus a 10–15 min daily nervous-system reset (slow breathing or quiet reflection). Nothing else. Prove to your nervous system that
    calm is safe and consistent.



    W E E K 2 — R E G U L AT E E M O T I O N
    Add "name it to tame it" + response gap + worry journal
    5-min emotional check-ins through the day, a deliberate pause before reacting, and 10 min of worry journaling before bed. Sentinel brains sleep better when
    worries live on paper.



    WEEK 3 — REBUILD RECOVERY
    Add one weekly "true rest" session + energy pacing
    90 minutes with no productivity, no input, no goal — a bath, a slow walk, music. Through the week, alternate high-effort and lighter tasks so the brain isn't
    overloaded. It will feel uncomfortable. That's the point.



    W E E K 4 — G E N T L E A C T I VAT I O N
    Goal-activation routine + spaced repetition
    Each morning: one small task-start routine + one intention + one thing you're looking forward to. Trains the underactive left-frontal pathway. Add spaced
    repetition for anything you're learning.




    After 30 days
    Repeat the qEEG. The markers most likely to shift first: arousal, regeneration, excessive delta, right-frontal asymmetry — and with them, your stress
    and burnout scores.
