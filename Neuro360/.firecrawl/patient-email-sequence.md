**Patient Email Sequence**

*Registration • Reminder • Report Ready • Booking • Post-Session*

How to use this document

This document contains five ready-to-send email templates that cover the
full patient lifecycle, from registration through their first session
and beyond. Replace the placeholders in square brackets with your real
values before sending.

Placeholders to replace

- \[First Name\] --- patient\'s first name

- \[Company Name\] --- your clinic or brand name

- \[Coach Name\] --- assigned coach or specialist

- \[Service Name\] --- the booked service or session type

- \[Date & Time\] --- confirmed appointment date and time

- \[Location or Video Link\] --- physical address or video call URL

- \[View My Report\] / \[Book a Coach\] --- call-to-action button links

Email 1 --- Registration Confirmation

**Trigger:** Sent immediately after a patient completes registration

**Subject:** Welcome aboard --- your report is on the way

**Preheader:** You\'re in. Here\'s what happens in the next 24 hours.

Email body

Hi \[First Name\],

Thanks for registering with us --- you\'re officially in.

Your report is now being prepared. Please allow up to 24 hours for it to
land on your dashboard. We\'ll send you a separate email the moment
it\'s ready to view.

In the meantime, feel free to log in and explore. You can scroll around
your dashboard, browse general information, and get familiar with
what\'s available before your personalised insights arrive.

A few things you can do while you wait:

- Complete your profile if you haven\'t already

- Browse our knowledge library

- Read up on what your report will cover

If you have any questions before then, just reply to this email and
we\'ll be happy to help.

Talk soon,

The \[Company Name\] Team

The \[Company Name\] Team

Email 5 --- Forgot Password / Password Reset

**Trigger:** Sent automatically when a patient clicks \'Forgot
password\' on the login screen

**Subject:** Reset your password

**Preheader:** Use the link below to set a new password --- expires in
30 minutes.

Email body

Hi \[First Name\],

We received a request to reset the password for your \[Company Name\]
account. Click the link below to choose a new one:

👉 \[Reset My Password\]

For your security, this link will expire in 30 minutes. If it expires
before you use it, just head back to the login page and request another.

A few things to keep in mind:

- Choose a password you haven\'t used before

- Use at least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols

- Never share your password with anyone, including our team

Didn\'t request this? You can safely ignore this email --- your password
won\'t change unless you click the link above. If you\'re seeing
repeated reset emails you didn\'t ask for, please reply to let us know
so we can secure your account.

Stay safe,

The \[Company Name\] Team

Email 6 --- Post-Session Follow-Up

**Trigger:** Sent a few hours after a completed session with the coach

**Subject:** How was your session? We\'d love to hear

**Preheader:** A few reflections, your next steps, and how to keep the
momentum going.

Email body

Hi \[First Name\],

Thanks for showing up to your session with \[Coach Name\] today. We hope
it gave you something valuable to sit with.

Real change happens between sessions, in the small choices and
reflections that follow. Here are a few ways to keep the momentum:

- Take five minutes to note one insight from today\'s session

- Pick one small action you can take this week

- Revisit your report --- you\'ll often see it differently after a
  session

If you\'d like to share quick feedback on how the session went, just
reply to this email --- we read every response, and it helps us serve
you better.

Ready to book your next session whenever you are:

👉 \[Book a Coach\]

Here for whatever\'s next,

The \[Company Name\] Team
