# NeuroSense360 - Complete Features Summary

## 🧠 Platform Overview
**NeuroSense360** (Limitless Brain Wellness by Dr. Sweta Adatia) is a comprehensive brain health platform combining clinical qEEG assessments, AI-powered analysis, personalized protocols, coaching, and progress tracking.

---

## 👥 User Roles & Dashboards

### Super Admin
- Manage clinics (approve, activate, deactivate)
- View all patient reports across system
- Track payments and subscriptions
- System-wide analytics dashboard
- Algorithm data processor
- Branding configuration (white-label)
- Alert monitoring
- Agreement management

### Clinic Admin
- Patient management (add, edit, delete)
- Upload and view patient reports
- Generate clinical reports
- Subscription management
- Usage tracking (reports used vs allocated)
- Clinic analytics
- Care plan generator

### Patient
- View brain wellness reports
- Access clinical assessments
- Book coaching appointments
- Track progress and milestones
- Educational resources library
- Journey timeline
- Download reports

## 🎯 Core Features

### 1. Brain Assessments
**5 Evidence-Based Assessment Types:**
- ADHD Rating Scale (18 questions, 15 min)
- GAD-7 Anxiety Scale (7 questions, 5 min)
- Perceived Stress Scale PSS-10 (10 questions, 10 min)
- Memory & Cognitive Assessment (6 tasks, 20 min)
- Mood & Emotional Regulation (5 sections, 12 min)

**Features:**
- Scientifically validated tools
- Personalized insights
- Progress tracking over time
- Complete assessment suite

### 2. qEEG Brain Mapping
- 15-minute clinical-grade brain scan
- BMW (Brain & Mental Wellness) Report with 13+ parameters
- Brain performance measurement
- qEEG file upload management
- Visual brain mapping
- Normative database comparison

### 3. Coaching Services
**Three Coaching Types:**
- **Brain Coaching** - Focus, executive function, cognitive performance ($80+/session)
- **Nervous System Coaching** - Stress regulation, trauma recovery ($90+/session)
- **qEEG Consultation** - Brain mapping, neurofeedback ($150+/session)

**Coach Features:**
- Specialty areas (ADHD, Memory, Stress, Wellness)
- Ratings and session counts
- Availability calendar
- Bio and credentials
- Direct booking system

### 4. Progress Tracking
- Weekly/Monthly performance trends
- Multiple metrics (Overall, Focus, Memory, Mood)
- Milestone achievements
- Goal tracking with progress bars
- Performance insights
- Best time analysis
- Achievement gamification

### 5. Content Library
**Content Types:**
- Articles - Educational brain health content
- Videos - Guided exercises and tutorials
- Podcasts - Expert interviews
- Exercises - Interactive brain training
- Research - Latest scientific findings

**Features:**
- Curated collections (Beginner, Advanced, etc.)
- Premium and free content
- Search and filtering
- Ratings and view counts
