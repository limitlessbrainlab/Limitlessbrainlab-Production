What is NeuroSense360?

NeuroSense360 is an online platform for brain health. It helps doctors and clinics check how a person's brain is performing using EEG scans, and gives easy-to-understand reports with suggestions to improve brain health.

Think of it like this: A patient gets a brain scan at a clinic. The clinic uploads the scan to NeuroSense360. The software uses AI to read the scan, create a detailed report, and suggest ways to improve brain health. The patient can then log in and see their report online.

Security & Data Protection

  • Passwords are encrypted - No one can see a user's password, not even the admin
  • Data is private - Clinics can only see their own patients. Patients can only see their own reports.
  • Secure payments - All payments go through Razorpay or Stripe (bank-level security)
  • HTTPS encryption - All data transfer is encrypted
  • Access logs - The system tracks who accessed what and when
  • Email verification - Accounts must verify email before they can be used
